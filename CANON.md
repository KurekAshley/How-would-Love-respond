# The Kurek Canon

## Purpose

The Kurek Canon is the official body of intellectual property, teachings, frameworks, methodologies, distinctions, stories, principles, and transformational systems developed by Kurek Ashley.

The purpose of the Canon is to preserve, organize, expand, and transmit this body of work in a structured and enduring format.

This repository serves as part of a larger ecosystem of interconnected repositories that together form the complete Kurek Canon.

---

# Vision

To create a comprehensive and enduring body of transformational knowledge that empowers individuals to create greater happiness, fulfillment, purpose, contribution, abundance, and love.

The Canon exists to preserve these teachings for future generations while providing a structured framework for continued growth and development.

---

# The Central Question

At the center of the entire Canon sits a single transformational question:

> How Would Love Respond?

This question acts as the philosophical foundation upon which all other frameworks are built.

Every major teaching within the Canon ultimately supports, expands, or applies this central principle.
