# Credibility

## Purpose

This document provides context for the experience, achievements, lessons, and real-world application behind the teachings contained within the **Kurek Canon**.

The principles, frameworks, and distinctions contained throughout this body of work were not created in theory alone. They were developed through decades of experience, observation, coaching, leadership, adversity, personal transformation, and practical application.

The purpose of this document is not self-promotion.

Its purpose is to establish the foundation from which these teachings emerged.

# Credibility Statement

The credibility of any teaching should not be measured solely by accomplishments.

It should be measured by:

- The lives improved
- The lessons learned
- The principles tested
- The adversity overcome
- The results produced
- The integrity of the work
- The willingness to continually live the principles being taught

This Canon represents a lifetime devoted to understanding and living one fundamental question:

> **How Would Love Respond?**
