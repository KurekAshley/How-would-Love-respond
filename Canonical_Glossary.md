# Canonical Glossary

## Purpose

The Canonical Glossary serves as the official source of truth for the definitions, terminology, concepts, frameworks, and language used throughout the Kurek Canon.

Its purpose is to ensure consistency across:

- Books
- Courses
- Workshops
- Coaching Programs
- Speaking Engagements
- Podcasts
- Interviews
- Community Platforms
- Film Projects
- Educational Materials
- Future Repositories

Whenever a term is defined within this glossary, that definition should be considered the authoritative version unless specifically superseded.

---

# A

## Abundance

The natural state of unlimited possibility, creativity, contribution, growth, opportunity, and fulfillment available to every individual.

Abundance is not merely financial wealth. It includes health, relationships, purpose, happiness, love, impact, freedom, and contribution.

### Related Concepts

- Love
- Purpose
- Contribution
- Formula for Creation

# C

## Conscious Response

A deliberate choice made from awareness rather than automatic reaction.

A conscious response reflects personal responsibility and alignment with one's highest values.

### Related Concepts

- Love Response Model
- Responsibility

## Contribution

The act of using one's gifts, talents, experiences, and resources to create value for others.

Contribution is a primary source of fulfillment and meaning.

# E

## Empowerment

The process of reclaiming personal responsibility, choice, and creative power.

Empowerment occurs when an individual recognizes their ability to influence their experience of life through conscious thought, emotion, belief, and action.

### Related Concepts

- Responsibility
- Personal Power
- Transformation

## Evolve or Dissolve

A foundational principle stating that nothing remains static.

Growth is required for continued expansion.

> In life either you evolve or else you dissolve. Nothing stays the same.

### Related Concepts

- Transformation
- Growth
- Purpose
